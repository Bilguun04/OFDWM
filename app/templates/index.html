About us

At OFDWM, we are committed to leveraging cutting-edge technology to improve wildfire response and firefighter deployment. Our mission is to develop an AI-powered system that enhances decision-making in emergency situations, ensuring that resources are allocated efficiently and lives are protected.

Program Overview

Using advanced machine learning models, real-time data processing, and an intuitive interface, we provide first responders with actionable insights to optimize firefighter availability, scheduling, and deployment. Our team is dedicated to building a solution that not only improves response times but also helps mitigate wildfire risks for communities worldwide.
